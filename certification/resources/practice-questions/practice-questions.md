# DP-700 Practice Questions

Domain-specific practice questions to test your knowledge before the exam. Every question is sourced from facts already covered in this guide's topic files — if you miss one, the fix is always to re-read the linked section, not to memorize the question itself.

## Difficulty Legend

| Tag | Meaning |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Easy** | Direct recall of a single documented fact or default value |
| **Medium** | Requires connecting two related facts, or reading a short scenario to identify the right mechanism |
| **Hard** | Multi-step reasoning, an edge case, or a scenario where a plausible-sounding distractor is easy to pick by mistake |

## How to Use

1. Attempt each question before revealing the answer — resist the urge to peek first.
2. Track your score per domain to identify weak areas; a domain scoring below 70% needs a re-read, not just more questions.
3. For every miss, re-read the linked topic file's relevant section (the explanation names it) rather than just memorizing the correct letter.
4. Pay special attention to **Common Mistake** callouts referenced in explanations — they're the most frequently tested traps in this guide.
5. Aim for 75%+ per domain before attempting the mock exams.
